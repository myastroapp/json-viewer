What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled, meaningless Latin-like text used as a placeholder in design and development since the 1500s. It lets you see how a layout looks with text in it without the distraction of readable content — useful for wireframes, templates, CMS previews, and component demos. This generator builds it from the classic word set, in your browser.
