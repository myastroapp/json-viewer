Privacy Policy — JSON Viewer & Formatter

Last updated: 15 June 2026

JSON Viewer & Formatter is built to be private by design. It runs entirely on your device and is intended to handle your data locally, in your browser, only.

What we collect

Nothing. The extension does not collect, store on any server, transmit, share, or sell any personal information or the contents of the JSON you view. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

How your data is handled

All parsing, formatting, searching, and rendering happens locally in your browser. The JSON you open is never sent anywhere by this extension. The extension makes no network requests of its own. (If you choose the optional “fetch from a URL” feature, your browser requests that URL directly, exactly as if you had typed it into the address bar; the extension does not see or route that traffic through any third party.)

Permissions and why they are used

  • activeTab — lets the extension read the current tab's text, only at the moment you click the toolbar icon, so it can format the JSON shown there.
  • scripting — used after that click to read the page's text for formatting.
  • storage — stores your preferences and briefly passes a page's JSON to the viewer tab, locally on your device.

Third parties

None. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
