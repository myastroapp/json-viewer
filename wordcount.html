About the counts

Words are runs of non-whitespace characters; characters are counted both with and without spaces (handy for Twitter/X, SMS, or meta-description limits). Sentences are split on . ! ?, paragraphs on blank lines, and reading time assumes ~200 words per minute (average adult silent reading). It's UTF-8 aware, so accents and non-Latin scripts count correctly, and it runs entirely on your device.
